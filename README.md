# 🌌 OmniSync Hub: The Nexus of Financial Intelligence (Genesis Imperial)

**OmniSync Hub** is the ultimate centralized financial ecosystem (Monorepo), powered by a team of **24 AI Experts** operating on a flexible Hybrid infrastructure (AMD/Spark for Lab & NVIDIA GB200/Dell for Production).

This project consolidates all distributed financial services into a unified whole, optimizing performance through the **Imperial AI (All-V3 Local)** strategy and **Zero-Cost Waterfall API**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

-   **🏰 22-Domain Monorepo:** Full coverage across every financial and enterprise vertical: Banking, Trading, Crypto, ERP, CRM, PropTech, ESG, and more.
-   **🤖 24 Specialized Experts:** A multi-tier collaborative system of 1 CEO (Llama 405B) and 23 DeepSeek-V3-grade AI staff.
-   **👑 Imperial Architecture:** Harnessing the power of **NVIDIA GB200 NVL72** (72 GPUs) in Production to run 671B models locally with zero latency.
-   **🌊 Hybrid R&D Lab:** Leverages NVIDIA Spark (AI) and AMD (Service) clusters for Lab/Dev/UAT environments to optimize research costs.
-   **⚡ Backend Trio:** The perfect combination of **Go** (Services), **Rust** (Performance), and **Python** (AI/ML).
-   **📱 Universal UI:** A single codebase in **Flutter** serving Mobile, Web, and Desktop.

## 🛠️ Quick Start

The project uses a Monorepo structure. All operations are centralized at the root:

1.  **Initialize the environment:** `.\platform\scripts\setup-env.bat`
2.  **Run the ecosystem demo flow:** `node platform/scripts/demo_ecosystem_flow.js`
3.  **Operate AI Agents:** See [agent_setup_guide.md](brain/agent_setup_guide.md)
